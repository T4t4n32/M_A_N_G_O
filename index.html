M.A.N.G.O. — Open, low-cost ocean monitoring

Bring environmental data to communities, researchers and decision-makers.

We build simple, robust devices and an open stack so anyone can collect, visualize and share oceanic data (levels, temperature, salinity). Explore docs, view public visualizations or request access to raw device data.
